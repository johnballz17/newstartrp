Community standards

Gathering random people from across the world to play cards, shoot zombies and play cops and robbers with.

At New Start RP, we don't believe in the hide the salami and ban someone if they give you trouble, baloney. We believe in second chances. We believe that players can grow with the community. Rather than kicking them out we smother them with kindness. Give them second chances. Another kick at the can.

Read Our Fundamentals Community Guidelines

Our Foundation

A healthy community starts with the way people treat one another. So, remember you can talk all the smack you want but be ready to have it dished out to you as well. And if you can't handle that then play somewhere else.

  • Ability to talk great smack Every good day starts with some great insults.
  • Consistency matters Don't pretend you are something you are not.
  • People over ego We value teamwork, humility, and solutions over drama, pride, or power struggles.
  • If you ask a million questions and open a million tickets You will not get a million answers or replies to your tickets.
  • If you get pissed off and leave the conversation you lost already. If you get into an argument with other players and can't resolve it in a role-play manner and decided to open up a ticket against other player and get all hot and heavy. You'd better have strong reasons behind your ticket. Not liking someone is no reason to try to get them banned. Take the high ground, find a better solution or two. Don't leave the conversation after you engaged it.

Daily Fundamentals

These are the standards we want to carry into every conversation, every event, and every interaction.

  • Show up with a good attitude Make a comment or two. Stoke the fire of conversation. Don't blab on like a blabber mouth.
  • Be dependable Follow through, with your role-play ideas. Sticktoitiveness is contagious and persistence will pay off.
  • Help build the atmosphere Every member contributes to the quality of the expereince. The right mixture of role-play personalities can have the ability to combine into a sweet awesome sauce that players will remember for years to come.

What We Aspire To

It is not about perfection, its about progress.

  • Lead by example The best way to inspire others is to show them that seemingly impossible things can be accomplished. After whats was said couldn't be done is done. Inspire others to do the same, become more and trade up.
  • Make room for patience Mistakes can become learning moments when handled with integral thinking.
  • This is not a safe zone for ideas Ideas are meant to be poked and prodded, until nothing is left. Ideas are not something to have then enforce without going through the hypothesis ringer. They have to be tested again and again. Ideas are good in nature but they have to stand up to the trail and error. If you have an idea and haven't thought it all the way through. Every contingency plan without proper thought is just another balled up paper in the trash. SO, what we apsire to...is...to....do...better when better is out there trying to do best, so we just have to aspire to out better the best.

Community Guidelines

  • Treat everyone with dignity No harassment, bullying, hate, or needless disrespect. We expect members to not be mean for the sake of being mean.
  • Calm or Conflict you decide Not every disagreement needs to become a fight. Step back, breathe, and handle issues constructively.
  • Keep the environment welcoming New members should feel like they are entering a community, not a clique.
  • Take accountability If you make mistake, own up to it and then move passed it and be better next time.
  • You can't always get what you want You can't win them all...all the time. But if you try some times you'll get a lamer version of what you originally wanted in the first place. They will call it getting what you need, but in reality you settled for something far less. Never settle, never fill up the mental fridge full of food. Stay hungry.
  • Support the mission If you've got an idea, some insight or inclination that it can be done better say something, so we can make improvements.
  • Value and Endure Value failure and endure the lows to reach the peaks. Enduring failure is what will spur you on to fight harder for success.
  • Encourage others Good communities build other people's character up, not knock them down.
  • Leave things better Wherever you go, whatever you do, leaves things better than when you found it.
Our goal is simple: have a good time, collaborating with friends and family.
