What Makes a Strong Roleplay Community? 🤝

It is having inside knowledge of the community. It is having history with the players and with the server. A strong Role play community sustains momentum and continues to get better over time. When you find what helps the community stay in the zone. Letting players find their own paths and make their own stories. Having those stories, evolve, adapt and intertwine with other players stories. Having inside jokes and stories is the glue that binds the community together.🌤️
